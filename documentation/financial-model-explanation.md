## Assumptions

The model begins with clearly defined assumptions including:

- Revenue growth rate
- Cost of goods sold percentage
- Operating expense growth
- Capital expenditure schedule
- Depreciation assumptions
- Working capital ratios

All assumptions drive the forecasting engine.

## Income Statement Logic

Revenue is projected based on growth assumptions.

COGS is calculated as a percentage of revenue.

Operating expenses are projected based on structured escalation rates.

EBITDA is derived from revenue minus operating expenses.

Net income flows into retained earnings on the Balance Sheet.

---

## Cash Flow Construction

Net Income  
+ Depreciation  
- Changes in Working Capital  
- Capital Expenditure  

= Net Cash Flow

The model follows the indirect method.
